## Social Objective
Use social channels to make `WE OUT HERE` feel culturally alive, recognizable, premium, and shareable without flattening it into generic topical content.

## Channel Priorities
- Priority 1: Instagram, X, TikTok
- Priority 2: Bluesky, newsletter cross-promotion, Discord announcements
- Priority 3: LinkedIn for industry legitimacy and collaborator visibility

## Social Positioning Line
`WE OUT HERE` is the dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy for anyone who has watched a group chat become a company and known that nothing good could come of it.

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Premise Recognition
Content that immediately communicates the setup.

Examples:
- "Three burned-out technologists started an AI consultancy on Backchannel."
- short clips of pitch, panic, or faux-professional behavior
- posters and teasers with compact hook lines

### Pillar 2: Character Recognition
Content that makes the trio legible and memorable.

Examples:
- character cards
- scene clips showing social function and damage pattern
- quote graphics tied to twinkle.puss, bl0ng, or ariane.emory

### Pillar 3: Workplace And Platform Truth
Content that viewers share because it feels painfully familiar.

Examples:
- meeting language clips
- deck jokes
- captions about work theater and confidence theater
- faux-document fragments if visually polished

### Pillar 4: Premium Identity
Content that signals the project is serious and aesthetically controlled.

Examples:
- key art
- stills
- trailer frames
- creator statements
- press pull quotes

### Pillar 5: Social Proof And Momentum
Content that shows the project is moving.

Examples:
- festival announcements
- press coverage
- team milestones
- launch countdowns
- partner or collaborator endorsements

## Platform-Specific Strategy

### X

#### Objective
Drive quick premise recognition, clip circulation, and industry-visible momentum.

#### Best Formats
- one-line premise posts
- clip plus caption
- announcement posts
- press quote reposts
- short threads for milestone context

#### Copy Strategy
- write compressed, dry, high-signal lines
- avoid hashtags unless event-specific and useful
- treat the first sentence as the whole point
- if posting a clip, caption it with one observation or one line of framing only

#### Posting Rhythm
- 3-5 posts per week during steady state
- 2-4 posts per day during launch windows

### Bluesky

#### Objective
Build cultural credibility and engage tastemakers in a slightly more reflective register.

#### Best Formats
- thoughtful launch notes
- stills with concise context
- press links with framing
- community-gratitude posts

#### Copy Strategy
- slightly more complete sentences than X
- give enough context to locate the show culturally
- use this platform for intention, not hype

#### Posting Rhythm
- 2-4 posts per week
- 1-2 extra posts around milestones

### Instagram

#### Objective
Present `WE OUT HERE` as a polished, desirable, distinctive series object.

#### Best Formats
- stills carousels
- teaser and trailer reels
- character cards
- quote cards
- posters and review graphics

#### Copy Strategy
- keep captions concise and controlled
- first line must work as a standalone hook
- use carousels to sequence information visually rather than explain everything in caption copy
- include clear CTA when appropriate: watch, sign up, join, read more

#### Posting Rhythm
- 3 feed posts or reels per week
- stories as needed around milestones and reminders

### TikTok

#### Objective
Drive discovery through immediate behavioral recognition and clip-native humor.

#### Best Formats
- subtitled scene clips
- creator-facing framing clips
- tight montages of quotes and reactions
- faux artifact or screen capture if visually clear and funny fast

#### Copy Strategy
- hook in first line and first frame
- use plain language over polished promo phrasing
- prioritize recognition over explanation
- let comments become signal for future clip selection

#### Posting Rhythm
- 3-6 posts per week during active rollout
- increase volume during launch and clip cycles

### LinkedIn

#### Objective
Signal credibility to industry peers, collaborators, and partners.

#### Best Formats
- milestone updates
- creator or team announcement posts
- festival or press announcements
- behind-the-project reflections

#### Copy Strategy
- professional, specific, unsentimental
- no inflated founder rhetoric
- show progress, team quality, and positioning discipline

#### Posting Rhythm
- 1-2 posts per week
- additional posts for major milestones only

### Discord Announcements

#### Objective
Keep the highest-intent community informed and involved.

#### Best Formats
- announcement posts
- event reminders
- AMA notices
- asset drops
- launch countdowns

#### Copy Strategy
- direct and actionable
- always include what changed, why it matters, and the next step
- if using @everyone or @here, reserve for genuinely significant updates

#### Posting Rhythm
- 1-2 major announcements per week maximum
- more frequent only during launch events or live activations

### Newsletter Cross-Promotion

#### Objective
Use social to drive signups and use newsletter excerpts to deepen social meaning.

#### Copy Strategy
- social posts should tease the editorial value of the newsletter, not just say "subscribe"
- use lines like "today's note is about why this show had to be made now" or "this week's update includes the first look and launch timeline"

## Recommended Content Mix
- 30% premise and hook content
- 25% clips and quote-led recognition content
- 15% visual identity and premium stills
- 15% momentum and milestone content
- 10% creator or process framing
- 5% direct community asks

## Editorial Rules
- never sound like a corporate streamer account
- never explain the joke after posting the clip
- never depend on trend formats that clash with the project's tone
- preserve the distinction between polished launch assets and looser conversational posts
- every platform should feel native, but the project should still feel like the same show everywhere

## Repurposing Logic
One major asset should yield multiple versions:

- trailer -> Instagram reel, TikTok cut, X clip, Bluesky still plus note, LinkedIn milestone post, newsletter lead image, Discord announcement
- press quote -> review graphic, X quote post, Bluesky link post, newsletter roundup block
- stills set -> Instagram carousel, X image post, website news update, LinkedIn visual post

## KPI Focus By Platform
- X: shares, profile visits, link clicks
- Bluesky: reposts, comments from tastemakers, site clicks
- Instagram: saves, shares, reel completion, profile taps
- TikTok: watch time, shares, comments, follows
- LinkedIn: qualified profile views, collaborator inquiries, message replies
- Discord: click-through rate, event attendance, reaction volume
- Newsletter promotion: signup conversion by source

## Social Operating Cadence
1. Plan weekly themes.
2. Build platform-specific versions, not copy-paste duplicates.
3. Track which hooks outperform: work truth, line quote, character, visual polish, or milestone.
4. Double down on the strongest behavior-recognition clips.
5. Keep one month of evergreen posts ready for gaps.
