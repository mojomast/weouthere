## Recruiting Messaging Principles
- sound serious, specific, and respectful
- lead with project identity and fit
- avoid desperate or vague "join our movement" language
- make the ask clear and bounded
- preserve the premium comedy positioning of the project

## Core Recruiting Boilerplate
`WE OUT HERE` is a 6 x 30 min dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy about three burned-out technologists who launch a chaotic AI consultancy from `Backchannel`. We are building the project with collaborators who understand sharp comedy, contemporary work culture, and internet-native storytelling.
