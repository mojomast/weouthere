## Calendar Assumption
This calendar assumes a major public reveal in Week 7. Shift dates as needed while keeping the sequence intact.

## Weekly Rollout Table

| Week | Theme | Main Goal | Core Assets | Main Channels |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Presence setup | establish public identity | bios, landing page, signup CTA | website, Instagram, X, Bluesky, LinkedIn |
| 2 | Premise seeding | build recognition | hook posts, still fragments | X, Bluesky, Instagram, TikTok |
| 3 | Tone proof | signal quality | quote cards, mood stills | Instagram, X, newsletter |
| 4 | Credibility beat | show motion | team or milestone update | LinkedIn, newsletter, website |
| 5 | Audience hook | expand curiosity | clip or short teaser | TikTok, Instagram, X |
| 6 | Countdown | prepare reveal | countdown graphics, reminder copy | all social, Discord, newsletter |
| 7 | Major reveal | concentrate attention | trailer or first look, press outreach | all channels |
| 8 | Sustain 1 | extend launch spike | quote cards, review lines, clip | X, Instagram, Bluesky, newsletter |
| 9 | Sustain 2 | deepen audience connection | character cards, BTS post | Instagram, TikTok, Discord |
| 10 | Press momentum | reinforce seriousness | roundup, interview, feature | website, newsletter, LinkedIn |
| 11 | Community activation | retain interest | AMA or event announcement | Discord, newsletter, socials |
| 12 | Next-step framing | bridge into next phase | recap and what's next | newsletter, website, X, LinkedIn |

## Week-By-Week Detail

### Week 1
- update website with synopsis and signup
- lock all social bios
- publish 1 introductory post on Instagram, X, Bluesky
- publish 1 LinkedIn post framing the project professionally

### Week 2
- publish 2-3 premise-led posts
- test short hook lines on X and Bluesky
- share one visual teaser on Instagram
- post one recognition-led TikTok setup or still-motion piece

### Week 3
- release first quote card set
- send introductory newsletter
- share tone-framing post on Bluesky
- post one polished still carousel on Instagram

### Week 4
- publish collaborator or production milestone
- send outreach note to select press and programmers
- publish LinkedIn update with clear project framing
- update website news section

### Week 5
- drop short clip or teaser fragment
- post TikTok-first version, then adapt to Instagram and X
- publish one Discord announcement for community members
- monitor which hook performs best

### Week 6
- begin reveal countdown
- send newsletter reminder that something major is coming
- post countdown graphic or caption series
- prep press materials and launch-day inboxes

### Week 7
- publish hero asset everywhere in coordinated sequence
- execute press email wave
- send launch-day newsletter
- post Discord announcement with direct links
- update website homepage or news page
- follow with second-wave social posts later the same day

### Week 8
- share strongest quote card
- publish one short clip from reveal asset
- repost or excerpt first coverage
- send short follow-up newsletter if response is strong

### Week 9
- publish character sequence across Instagram and TikTok
- run Bluesky post about character dynamics or tone
- post community-facing update in Discord

### Week 10
- package press responses into a roundup
- update website with coverage log
- publish LinkedIn credibility post for industry audience
- keep one audience-facing clip in circulation

### Week 11
- announce AMA, livestream, screening, or Q&A if available
- use Discord as primary event coordination hub
- support with newsletter and socials

### Week 12
- send recap newsletter
- publish website update summarizing momentum
- post concise thread or post about next steps
- refresh outreach list based on who engaged during Weeks 7-11

## Monthly Rhythm After Initial 12 Weeks
- 1 major newsletter per month
- 1-2 LinkedIn updates per month
- weekly Instagram / X / TikTok content if new assets exist
- Discord announcements only when there is real value

## Publicity Asset Sequence
1. identity post
2. hook posts
3. stills and quote cards
4. short teaser or clip
5. countdown assets
6. major reveal asset
7. quote, review, and reaction assets
8. character and BTS assets
9. recap and next-step messaging

## Press And Outreach Overlay
- pre-brief selected contacts in Weeks 4-6
- full outreach wave in Week 7
- follow-up wave in Weeks 8-10 with social proof and responses
- long-tail outreach in Weeks 11-12 based on traction

## Success Markers By Phase
- Weeks 1-3: brand clarity and early signups
- Weeks 4-6: proof of seriousness and rising anticipation
- Weeks 7-8: attention spike and press coverage
- Weeks 9-12: retention, legitimacy, and next-step conversion
