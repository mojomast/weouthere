Jobs / Join the Production

A lean indie professional production looking for sharp, collaborative operators.

Why Work On This

WE OUT HERE is built for people who like precision, speed, strong taste, and clear communication. The production is designed to be ambitious without pretending chaos is a virtue.

Key Roles

Line Producer

Own the practical build of the project: budget, schedule, staffing, and production workflows.

Production Manager

Run prep and day-to-day production logistics, vendors, calendars, and operational coordination.

Casting Director

Lead the strategy and process for identifying performers who can carry the show’s tonal precision.

Director

Translate the scripts into a shootable visual and performance language across desktop realism, live action, and surreal inserts.

Working Style

Hybrid development and prep. Clear expectations. Respectful collaboration. No fake-open roles, no ambiguity around responsibilities, no treating burnout as proof of seriousness.
