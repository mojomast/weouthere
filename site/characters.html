Characters

Not symbols. People trying to survive.

The characters of WE OUT HERE are not symbols for internet culture. They are people trying to survive inside it, leverage it, or weaponize it without getting spiritually hollowed out in the process.

twinkle.puss (The Face)

Role in ecosystem: Charismatic Frontman / Liability

Fluent in meme-native rhetoric and manic hustle. Uses speed and intuition as moral cover. Terrified of being seen as ordinary, constantly pre-announcing a reality that doesn't exist yet.

bl0ng (The Architecture)

Role in ecosystem: Technical Implementer / Enabler

Ethically detached, technically proficient. Treats human interaction as a structural engineering problem. The person actually hardcoding the "paradigm" while everyone else talks about it.

ariane.emory (The Superego)

Role in ecosystem: Operations / Desperate Adult

Craves legitimacy, structure, and traceability. Constantly seeking an authority figure or "VC parent" to adopt them. Her desire for containment is just another form of pathology.
