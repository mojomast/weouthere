Characters

Three people trying to turn fluency into safety.

twinkle.puss

The impulsive visionary. Magnetic, volatile, hilarious, socially dangerous, and frighteningly persuasive. He can turn humiliation into momentum and panic into brand language in real time. He is the engine behind Cataclysm Consulting: closer, instigator, myth-maker, and walking liability.

bl0ng

The failed superego. Dry, hyper-articulate, technically gifted, avoidant, funny, and chronically defended. He sees the danger first and still helps build the machine.

ariane.emory

The detached meta-observer. Precise, composed, emotionally acute, overfunctioning, and very funny in a dry, surgical register. She can see what is wrong before anyone else, but insight does not spare her from attachment, ambition, or complicity.

Group Dynamic

The trio function as a self-sealing emotional structure. Twinkle escalates, bl0ng reframes, ariane contains. Then the order flips and the compensations become the actual problem. Their friendship is adaptive, loving, exploitative, and unsustainable all at once.
