About the Series

Built around the emotional logic of startup culture.

WE OUT HERE is a premium indie dark sitcom built around the emotional logic of startup culture, internet self-construction, and the performance demands of modern creative life. The series tracks a tightly wound social world where work, friendship, ambition, romance, and identity management can no longer be separated cleanly.

The Premise

Everyone in this world is trying to become undeniable. Some want money. Some want relevance. Some want cultural legitimacy. Some just want to stop feeling replaceable.

The problem is that the same systems promising transcendence keep producing insecurity, self-surveillance, and weird new forms of intimacy. WE OUT HERE turns that pressure into comedy without letting anyone off the hook.

Tone & Texture

The show lives in the overlap between psychologically sharp character comedy, startup social realism, and internet-era dread. It is funny because the characters are serious. It is serious because they cannot stop performing.

Core Themes

  • Ambition as an identity system
  • Work replacing personality
  • Friendship under extreme competitive pressure
  • Online visibility resulting in self-erasure
  • Belonging, status, and emotional distortion
