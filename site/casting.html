Casting

Open casting. Performance-first. Smart, darkly funny, psychologically grounded.

This page is designed to give actors, reps, and collaborators a legitimate public route into the project without ambiguity or chaos.

Submission Guidance

Open casting. Performance-first. Strong interest in actors with theater rigor, indie screen naturalism, and dark-comedy control. Chemistry, listening, and tonal intelligence matter as much as individual heat.

Standard headshot, resume, reel, and relevant contemporary comedy/drama footage are useful. Self-tape instructions and sides are available in the full casting package.
