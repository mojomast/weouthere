FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to common questions about project status, submissions, materials, hiring, community, and contact.

What is the current status of the project?

The project has a production-ready package complete. It is currently in active development, securing partnerships, and structuring financing for the 6-episode season.

Are you accepting casting submissions?

Not publicly at this time. When casting opens, all roles and submission rules will be posted strictly on the Casting page.

How can I invest or partner with the production?

Qualified parties can review the Investors/Partners page and use the Request a Conversation link to speak with our producing team directly. We do not distribute financial materials unsolicited.

Is this a real startup or a show?

It is a premium indie television series. The line is meant to blur in the marketing, but fundamentally, WE OUT HERE is a 6x30 minute dark sitcom.
