FAQ

Direct answers to repeated questions.

What is WE OUT HERE?

A 6 x 30 minute single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy about three burned-out technologists who launch a chaotic AI consultancy from Backchannel.

What stage is the project in?

The full project package is complete and includes development, scripts, casting, press, website, finance, legal, production, post, distribution, recruiting, and ops materials.

Where can I find the press kit?

Use the press page on this site or the full doc in `06_press_kit/press_kit.md`.

Are you currently casting?

The public-facing casting package is prepared. Active role timing and submission windows should always be checked against the latest official notice.

Are there open jobs?

The jobs package is prepared. Hiring should route through the official production channel when active roles are posted.

How do I join the community?

The project plans to use a curated Discord community as an announcement-and-conversation layer, not a freeform chaos pit.
