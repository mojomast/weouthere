Community / Discord

A curated audience layer, not a chaos pit.

Community Thesis

WE OUT HERE should build a small but high-quality audience community that feels intentional, informed, and useful to the project’s growth, while preserving the series’ premium identity and preventing tone drift.

The real-world community hub is planned as a Discord space for announcements, updates, controlled conversation, and selective participation.

What Members Get

Earlier Context

Fuller context than public socials can provide.

Useful Updates

Major launch beats, events, festival news, and calls to action.

Boundaries

A moderated tone-consistent environment without freeform discourse spirals.

What This Is Not

Not a crowdwriting space. Not a substitute writers’ room. Not a fan-labor engine. Not a substitute for the website or newsletter.
