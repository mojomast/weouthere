For Investors / Partners

A disciplined indie package with more than one credible path to market.

Executive Summary

WE OUT HERE is a 6 x 30 min dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy designed as a contained, desktop-heavy hybrid production. The recommended season budget is $2.25M, with a target release path of festival premiere to streamer acquisition and a credible digital-first fallback.

Why the Budget Is Rational

Contained Production

Recurring interiors, a limited core cast, and efficient block shooting support higher production value per dollar.

Desktop-Heavy Form

Screenlife and UI storytelling create real efficiencies without pretending the work is free.

Multiple Revenue Paths

The project is not dependent on a single streamer greenlight scenario.

Planned Financing Mix

Base working mix: equity, tax incentive, soft money / grants, MG or pre-license, and producer deferrals. The full investor memo, waterfall, and scenario modeling live in the finance package.
