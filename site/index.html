About the Series

Built from pressure, performance, and failed containment.

This is a show about people trying to build meaning, leverage, intimacy, and selfhood inside systems that flatten all four. It understands startup language, online ambition, and self-branding from the inside, then turns that familiarity into character pressure rather than empty satire.

Three burned-out technologists launch a chaotic AI consultancy from Backchannel and discover that the same friendship structure keeping the company alive is also making everyone less stable.
