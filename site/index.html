Independent Series

WE OUT HERE is a dark sitcom about startup rot, social theater, and the cost of trying to become undeniable online.

A six-episode single-camera tech psychocomedy built from desktop realism, live action, and surreal insert sequences. Three burned-out technologists turn a Backchannel joke into Cataclysm Consulting and discover that the same fluency making them marketable is making them less stable.

Project Snapshot

Built as a real series, not a mood board.

WE OUT HERE tracks `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory` as they bootstrap legitimacy through manic hustle, pseudo-legal improvisation, ironic detachment, and meme-native fluency. The friendship that keeps the company alive is the same structure preventing anyone from stabilizing.

The season escalates in a fixed arc: mania, fragile success, ethical drift, containment failure, ironic collapse, and exposure to consequences.

Why This Project

Culturally Current

The show treats AI as a human systems problem: status, labor anxiety, fraudulence, self-narration, and consequence.

Formally Distinct

Readable desktop realism and live action are used as dramatic tools, not gimmicks, with controlled surreal inserts when reality breaches containment.

Production-Ready

The package includes scripts, casting, legal, finance, press, marketing, website, production, post, distribution, and recruiting materials.
