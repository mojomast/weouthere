Episodes

Six half-hours. One locked escalation spine.

Season one escalates social pressure, emotional fragmentation, and strategic self-presentation across one tightly linked ecosystem.

01. Don't Send That

A professional humiliation turns into a fake consultancy launch, then into a real liability the second the wrong message gets a serious reply.

02. Terms of Abuse

The trio discover that legitimacy means contracts, traceability, terms, and the panic of being formally discoverable.

03. The Manic Defense

What looked like hustle becomes ideology as speed, intuition, and anti-bureaucratic genius start functioning as moral cover.

04. Contain Me Daddy

The founders seek structure and oversight, only to reveal how much they want a parent instead of an actual system.

05. The Superego Collapse

Public damage strips away the group’s moral language and exposes the routines they’ve been using to excuse themselves.

06. Let Him Cook()

One last live demonstration aims to convert scandal into legitimacy and instead makes consequence undeniable.
