## Episode Title
"Contain Me Daddy"

## Logline
As workload and volatility start showing through the company's polished language, the trio pursue an adult authority figure to stabilize `Cataclysm Consulting`, only to reveal that what they actually want is not governance but permission with paternal branding.

## A/B/C Story Summary
- A Story: `Niko Stern` introduces the trio to `Dr. Liora Voss`, a performance consultant who could become advisor, operator, or therapist-adjacent stabilizer if they can survive intake.
- B Story: `ariane.emory` tries to formalize decision rights inside the company and discovers everyone wants containment only when it costs nothing.
- C Story: The phrase `contain me daddy` begins as a joke in chat and mutates into an embarrassing summary of the whole company dynamic.

## Theme / Psychodynamic Function
The episode exposes the fantasy underneath the group's talk about structure: they do not want ordinary accountability. They want a powerful witness who will bless the chaos and absorb the liability.

## Cold Open
The trio complete an advisor intake form together and cannot answer `What support do you need right now?` without sounding seductive, oppositional, or clinically evasive.

## Teaser
`Dr. Liora Voss` arrives as a possible container. Within minutes, the trio split into performance, resistance, and yearning.

## Full Act Structure
- Cold Open: The support question no one can answer.
- Teaser: `Dr. Liora Voss` enters as an authority figure with actual boundaries.
- Act One: Ariane proposes decision rights and escalation rules while the others immediately game them.
- Act Two: The intake session becomes a comic referendum on whether the trio can tolerate structure.
- Act Three: `Dr. Liora Voss` refuses the projected role; the group nullifies the last credible container.
- Tag: A crisis hits minutes after the adult leaves.

## Scene List
1. Cold open: advisor intake form.
2. Internal governance fight.
3. `Dr. Liora Voss` introduction.
4. Intake session.

9. Tag: new client pressure with no container left.

## Full Screenplay-Style Markdown Script

### COLD OPEN

**INT. BACKCHANNEL / SHARED FORM - MORNING**

Field on screen: `What support do you need right now?`

The cursor blinks. Nobody types.

**twinkle.puss**  
Can I say `loving but premium supervision`.

**ariane.emory**  
No.

**bl0ng**  
Can I say `a reduction in preventable entropy without ideological capture`.

**ariane.emory**  
Also no.

**twinkle.puss**  
What if we just write `adult with admin permissions`.

Beat.

`twinkle.puss` types privately to the group chat:

`contain me daddy`

`bl0ng` reacts with skull emoji. `ariane.emory` does not react.

TITLE: `WE OUT HERE`

### TEASER

### SCENE 1

**INT. ARIANE.EMORY'S APARTMENT - DAY**

Printed agenda on the table: `Internal Governance Pass`.

**ariane.emory**  
Before we meet `Dr. Liora Voss`, we need to decide whether we actually want help or just a witness with credentials.

**twinkle.puss**  
That sounds pejorative toward my process.

**bl0ng**  
I reject the binary.

`ariane.emory` flips the page.

**ariane.emory**  
Proposal. Client-facing promises require two yeses. Data access changes require explicit logging. No one sends major outbound messages after midnight.

**twinkle.puss**  
So we're replacing instinct with municipal sadness.

**bl0ng**  
Also time-based rules discriminate against insight.

**ariane.emory**  
You're both proving my point before lunch.

### ACT ONE

### SCENE 2

**INT. VIDEO CALL - AFTERNOON**

`Dr. Liora Voss` appears. Expensive calm. Perfect audio. The kind of face that makes people confess on sight and resent themselves for it.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Niko framed this as a high-functioning team under strain. I find that phrase usually means one person is on fire, one is translating the fire into language, and one is holding the extinguisher until her arm goes numb.

The trio sit still because being read that fast is vulgar.

**twinkle.puss**  
Okay, rude but chic.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
What do you want from me.

Nobody answers cleanly.

**bl0ng**  
Strategic containment.

**twinkle.puss**  
Calibration without deadening.

**ariane.emory**  
Decision structure.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Those are three different asks. Good.

### SCENE 3

**INT. ARIANE.EMORY'S APARTMENT - CONTINUOUS**

The intake session is part therapy, part org design, part hostage exchange.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Who can say no in this company and have it matter.

Silence.

**twinkle.puss**  
Emotionally or operationally.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Operationally.

**bl0ng**  
Then the answer is dynamic.

**ariane.emory**  
No one.

The room shifts. That was too plain.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Good. That's an answer.

**twinkle.puss**  
Can I defend us for one second before we get put down.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
You may.

**twinkle.puss**  
The reason we're effective is that we move before slower people can ritualize themselves into cowardice.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
And the cost.

**twinkle.puss**  
We get a little... texturally unstable.

**bl0ng**  
The governance layer is emergent.

**ariane.emory**  
The governance layer is me.

That lands harder than the joke deserves.

### SCENE 4

**INT. BACKCHANNEL - DURING INTAKE**

Private side chat between `twinkle.puss` and `bl0ng` while `Dr. Liora Voss` speaks.

`twinkle.puss`: i need her to tell me i'm intense but usable  
`bl0ng`: i need her not to turn us into linkedin adults  
`twinkle.puss`: contain me daddy  
`bl0ng`: never type that again  
`twinkle.puss`: impossible she just asked about authority and now my whole nervous system is doing branding

`ariane.emory` sees the side-chat notification reflected in Twinkle's glasses and hates that she knows what kind of joke it is.

### ACT TWO

### SCENE 5

**INT. VIDEO CALL - LATER**

`Dr. Liora Voss` brings up a simple framework on screen.

- decision rights
- red-line behaviors
- escalation protocol
- rest and response norms

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
If I were advising you, I'd start here. Who owns final client commitments.

**ariane.emory**  
Not twinkle.puss alone.

**twinkle.puss**  
Obsessed with how personal that felt.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Who owns risk assessment.

**bl0ng**  
Depends whether risk means legal, reputational, or metaphysical.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
This is what avoidance sounds like in expensive words.

`bl0ng` actually smiles. He likes her a little for that.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Who is allowed to interrupt momentum.

`ariane.emory` raises her hand a little, then drops it because the gesture itself feels pathetic.

**twinkle.puss**  
Can I ask a real question.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Please.

**twinkle.puss**  
How do you contain something without killing the thing that's good about it.

There it is. Not clean, but real.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
You stop treating containment as punishment. A boundary isn't a critique of aliveness. It's what keeps aliveness from becoming extraction.

Beat.

`twinkle.puss` looks down as if someone touched a bruise without warning.

Then he grins.

**twinkle.puss**  
So chic. Hate that.

### SCENE 6

**INT. HALLWAY - MOMENTS LATER**

`ariane.emory` and `Dr. Liora Voss` have a short side conversation while the others fake a snack emergency.

**ariane.emory**  
Can this work.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Do you want the answer that preserves hope or the answer that respects pattern.

**ariane.emory**  
Pattern.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
Not if they hire me as a parent and then punish me for parenting.

`ariane.emory` absorbs that because she already knew.

### ACT THREE

### SCENE 7

**INT. LIVING ROOM - EVENING**

Final ten minutes of the session.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
I'm not the right person to be your advisor in the form Niko described.

`twinkle.puss` stiffens instantly, making it a joke before it can become rejection.

**twinkle.puss**  
Sick. Love being too textured for services.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
That's not what I said. You do not need a charisma-friendly adult who absorbs consequence. You need real operating agreements and probably separate forms of support from one another.

**bl0ng**  
That feels administratively homophobic to the group vibe.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
It may be. It's still true.

**ariane.emory**  
Could you at least help us draft the agreements.

**Dr. Liora Voss**  
I can send a framework. I will not become the thing you can blame for not using it.

She exits the call with terrifying professionalism.

Silence.

### SCENE 8

**INT. APARTMENT - CONTINUOUS**

No one moves for a beat.

**twinkle.puss**  
She had a very expensive way of saying no one here gets to freestyle authority.

**bl0ng**  
I think technically she said we eroticize oversight.

**ariane.emory**  
She said we want a parent, not structure.

**twinkle.puss**  
That's such a mean way to describe chemistry.

**ariane.emory**  
You can't keep calling chaos chemistry because you like how it lights you.

That lands too hard. Twinkle goes still.

**twinkle.puss**  
And you can't keep acting like paperwork is love.

Beat.

There it is. The real fight. Small, exact, awful.

`bl0ng` looks between them, wanting to dissolve into language and failing.

**bl0ng**  
If I answer as a human being right now, things may worsen.

Nobody laughs.

### TAG

**INT. BACKCHANNEL - TEN MINUTES LATER**

Notification storm.

`Niko Stern`: `health-tech lead wants emergency call tonight. heard you all are freakishly good under pressure.`

`Jessa Vale`: `board asking for expanded scope tomorrow. can you send revised plan?`

`HexadecimalBoy`: `someone in public ai ops server asking if cataclysm has an advisor now lol`

The framework email from `Dr. Liora Voss` arrives unopened.

`twinkle.puss` starts typing.

`ariane.emory`: don't send that

Twinkle stops. Then keeps typing anyway.

Cut to black.

## Character Objective Tracking
- `twinkle.puss`: Find a form of authority that blesses his intensity without constraining it, then rage when that fantasy is denied.
- `bl0ng`: Preserve the group's exceptionalism and avoid any governance structure that would force him into plain accountability.
- `ariane.emory`: Import real limits before the company breaks beyond repair, then confront how much she also wanted rescue.

## Running Jokes and Callback Opportunities

- The intake should feel mostly like a real advisory consultation with comic contamination around the edges.
- The emotional truth moment is Twinkle asking how to contain something without killing it.
- Keep the final fight clipped and specific; no speeches.
