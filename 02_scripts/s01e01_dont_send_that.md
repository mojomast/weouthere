## Episode Title
"Don't Send That"

## Logline
After a professional humiliation, `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory` jokingly invent an AI consultancy on Backchannel, then accidentally turn the bit into a real business when the one message that absolutely should not be sent gets an eager reply.

## A/B/C Story Summary
- A Story: The trio send a reckless outreach message to exhausted founder `Jessa Vale` and must invent `Cataclysm Consulting` fast enough to survive the reply.
- B Story: `ariane.emory` tries to impose basic reality on the company's origin story while `bl0ng` converts panic into language that looks deliberate.
- C Story: The group's private shame about layoffs, irrelevance, and money flips into shared grandiosity the second an audience appears.

## Theme / Psychodynamic Function
Shame becomes manic purpose the moment it can be socialized. The episode dramatizes the founding defense of the season: if a humiliating reality can be renamed quickly enough, it can feel like destiny.

## Cold Open
At a low-budget AI panel, `twinkle.puss` is forced to answer what he actually does for work. Before he can admit he is between jobs, he says he runs a consultancy. In the private Backchannel chat, `bl0ng` and `ariane.emory` watch him lie into existence.

## Teaser
Back at `ariane.emory`'s apartment and in Backchannel, the trio keep the bit alive long enough to name the company, build fake roles, and draft an outrageous outbound message to `Jessa Vale`. `ariane.emory` says, correctly, "don't send that." It sends.

## Full Act Structure
- Cold Open: Public humiliation flips into performative invention.
- Teaser: The founding outreach message is sent and gets a response.
- Act One: The trio invent `Cataclysm Consulting` in one night: name, bios, service list, and a reasons-to-believe deck.
- Act Two: Their first client call goes unexpectedly well because `Jessa Vale` is desperate enough to value confidence over proof.
- Act Three: A proposal goes out, roles harden, and the trio experience the first intoxicating evidence that their bit may be economically real.
- Tag: A request for contracts arrives before they have any company infrastructure.

## Full Screenplay-Style Markdown Script

### COLD OPEN

**INT. COWORKING EVENT SPACE - DAY**

A tasteful banner reads: `AI NOW: PRACTICAL TRUST FOR MODERN TEAMS`.

The room is half full of founders, consultants, and people who look like they were promised wine. Onstage, a MODERATOR smiles the smile of someone who has never had to operationalize anything.

Split screen with a private Backchannel channel: `#panel-hate`.

`bl0ng` types from home. `ariane.emory` types from the back row.

**MODERATOR**  
And now we'd love to hear from members of the community. What are you building right now?

`twinkle.puss` is not meant to answer. He already raised his hand ironically and was rewarded with a microphone.

**twinkle.puss**  
Emotionally? A bunker. Professionally?

He clocks the room. The pause is too long.

Backchannel:

`bl0ng`: do not freestyle your employment status in public  
`ariane.emory`: you can just say independent strategist  
`bl0ng`: or medically mysterious

**twinkle.puss**  
I run a boutique AI consultancy.

The room perks up. A couple of heads turn. Someone writes that down.

Backchannel stops moving for a beat.

**MODERATOR**  
Amazing. Name?

Backchannel explodes.

`ariane.emory`: do not  
`bl0ng`: pick a noun and an apocalypse  
`ariane.emory`: twinkle.puss

**twinkle.puss**  
Cataclysm Consulting.

There is a low appreciative murmur from exactly the kind of people who should know better.

In the audience, `Jessa Vale`, polished and dead-eyed, glances up from her phone.

**MODERATOR**  
What do you specialize in?

**twinkle.puss**  
We help confused organizations adopt language models without spiritually flattening their people.

`bl0ng` types:

`bl0ng`: disgusting  
`bl0ng`: strong line though

**MODERATOR**  
Would love your card after.

`twinkle.puss` smiles like he has spent years preparing for this moment instead of ninety seconds.

CUT TO BLACK.

TITLE: `WE OUT HERE`

### TEASER

**EXT. COWORKING SIDE ALLEY - DAY**

`twinkle.puss` exits into harsh daylight, immediately hitting a vape that does not work.

`ariane.emory` follows, controlled in the way people get when homicide is off the table.

`ariane.emory`  
You cannot debut a company because a stranger gave you a microphone.

**twinkle.puss**  
Counterpoint, I just did.

`bl0ng` appears on speakerphone, tinny and delighted.

**bl0ng (PHONE)**  
The phrase `spiritually flattening their people` now has tenancy in my head.

**ariane.emory**  
We do not have a company.

**twinkle.puss**  
Jessa Vale looked at me like a drowning woman spotting driftwood made of jargon. We have a market.

**ariane.emory**  
You have a personality disorder with branding instincts.

**twinkle.puss**  
And yet. Market.

He checks his phone. `Jessa Vale` has posted on a public Backchannel industry server: `Need outside help untangling internal AI rollout. team is frozen between legal / support / product. DM if you've actually done this.`

`twinkle.puss` stops walking.

**twinkle.puss**  
She posted. Oh my God, she posted.

**ariane.emory**  
No.

**twinkle.puss**  
Yes.

**ariane.emory**  
No in the sense that I know your face right now.

### SCENE 2

**INT. ARIANE.EMORY'S APARTMENT - NIGHT**

Laptop glow. Delivery containers. A legal pad with nothing useful on it.

On Backchannel, a new private server exists: `cataclysm consulting`.

Channels:

- `#standing-on-business`
- `#deck-emergency`
- `#liability-sadness`
- `#money-prayer`

`twinkle.puss` has made a logo in a free design app. It is a comet made of serif text.

**bl0ng**  
It looks like a perfume that causes class action.

**twinkle.puss**  
Exactly. Premium risk.

**ariane.emory**  
If this is happening at all, we need one sentence that a sober person could say out loud.

`twinkle.puss` is already drafting the message.

On screen:

`Jessa - saw your note. we do emergency AI adoption triage for teams stuck between legal fear and executive magical thinking. can jump on this fast if you need adults who speak both compliance and delusion.`

**ariane.emory**  
Don't send that.

**bl0ng**  
The adult line is overreaching in a way I respect but cannot insure.

**twinkle.puss**  
I can make it colder.

He revises.

`We run rapid AI implementation and governance sprints for teams blocked by cross-functional mistrust. Happy to compare notes if useful.`

**bl0ng**  
Now it sounds like we kill at conferences.

**ariane.emory**  
We have never done any of those nouns.

**twinkle.puss**  
We've done the feelings under those nouns.

**ariane.emory**  
That is not how commerce works.

`twinkle.puss` hovers over send, savoring the power.

**ariane.emory**  
Don't send that.

**twinkle.puss**  
I love that you're framing me as the active agent here when destiny is very clearly mousing.

He clicks.

Silence.

Then: notification.

`Jessa Vale`: `Tomorrow 10am? Need capabilities deck before then if possible.`

No one moves.

**bl0ng**  
Well.

**ariane.emory**  
I need everyone to stop making the bit taxable.

**twinkle.puss**  
No, no. This is good. This is actually ideal. We're being selected.

**ariane.emory**  
By whom. For what.

**twinkle.puss**  
For relevance.

### ACT ONE

### SCENE 3

**INT. ARIANE.EMORY'S APARTMENT - LATER**

Whiteboard. Three names. No structure.

`twinkle.puss` writes titles beneath them.

`twinkle.puss`: Founder / Strategic Vision  
`bl0ng`: Systems Architect  
`ariane.emory`: Operations and Risk

**ariane.emory**  
I object to `and Risk` because it implies I agreed to inherit your endocrine system.

**bl0ng**  
I object to `architect` because if anyone asks for a build history I'm going to have to fake humility.

**twinkle.puss**  
We are role assigning retroactively to preserve the illusion of intention. Please be adults.

**ariane.emory**  
You're the one who said the company existed into a microphone.

**twinkle.puss**  
Yes. Leadership.

### SCENE 4

**INT. BACKCHANNEL / SHARED DOCS - NIGHT**

A shared doc called `capabilities_FINAL` opens.

Bullet points appear rapidly.

`bl0ng` typing: `AI readiness diagnostics`  
`twinkle.puss` typing: `narrative and adoption repair`  
`ariane.emory` typing: `cross-functional workflow design`

They all stop at the same time.

**bl0ng**  
That almost sounds like a firm.

**ariane.emory**  
That is because firms are often language plus upholstery.

**twinkle.puss**  
See? We're halfway there and we haven't even lied about a case study yet.

**ariane.emory**  
We are not inventing case studies.

**bl0ng**  
I can describe adjacent experience with enough sadness to imply confidentiality.

### SCENE 5

**INT. KITCHEN - NIGHT**

They work while standing because sitting would mean admitting duration.

`twinkle.puss` dictates bios like a royal fraud.

**twinkle.puss**  
Mine should imply repeated proximity to power but no subpoenaable titles.

**ariane.emory**  
You want the bio to be impressive and useless.

**twinkle.puss**  
The ideal male credential: proximity without liability.

**bl0ng**  
My bio needs to say I understand machine learning without inviting anyone to ask where.

**ariane.emory**  
You can say `technical strategy across research and product environments.`

**bl0ng**  
That's hot. That's exactly true nowhere.

They all laugh.

Then, unexpectedly, the laugh thins.

**ariane.emory**  
I can do mine.

She types: `ariane.emory has led operations, trust, and risk-adjacent programs across fast-moving teams.`

**twinkle.puss**  
Risk-adjacent is beautiful. It says you were near the fire with a clipboard.

**ariane.emory**  
That's my brand.

### SCENE 6

**INT. ARIANE.EMORY'S BEDROOM / SURREAL INSERT - NIGHT**

The deck multiplies across the screen. New versions bloom like fungal growth.

`capabilities_FINAL`  
`capabilities_FINAL_real`  
`capabilities_FINAL_v4`  
`capabilities_USE_THIS`

Surreal insert: the filenames orbit `twinkle.puss`' head like a crown made of notifications.

**twinkle.puss**  
Can we make one slide that says every company is running a group project with procurement and fear?

**bl0ng**  
Only if the next slide says we resolve fear at workflow level.

**ariane.emory**  
No sentence containing `resolve fear` is leaving this apartment.

### ACT TWO

### SCENE 7

**INT. ARIANE.EMORY'S APARTMENT - MORNING**

Nobody slept. The apartment has the spiritual profile of an airport gate.

Backchannel pings from a new user: `HexadecimalBoy`: `saw cataclysm server icon in sidebar. hard.`

**twinkle.puss**  
Why is there already a fan.

**bl0ng**  
Because you named the company like a nightclub for consultants.

**ariane.emory**  
Please do not onboard civilians before the first call.

### SCENE 8

**INT. VIDEO CALL - DAY**

The trio appear in separate little rectangles. `twinkle.puss` has staged a lamp. `bl0ng` has not staged himself at all. `ariane.emory` looks accidentally employable.

`Jessa Vale` joins from a tasteful office that feels expensive and doomed.

**Jessa Vale**  
Thank you for jumping on this. Sorry if my post was slightly feral.

**twinkle.puss**  
Those are our preferred market signals.

**ariane.emory**  
We work best when there's a concrete blockage. What exactly is stuck?

`Jessa Vale` exhales. The relief is immediate.

**Jessa Vale**  
Leadership wants `AI integration`. Legal wants us to say no to everything. Support is already using tools off-book. Product thinks this is a messaging issue. I need a pilot that gives everyone a sentence they can live with.

`bl0ng` wakes up visibly.

**bl0ng**  
So you don't have a tooling problem. You have a coordination and permission problem wearing a tooling costume.

`Jessa Vale` points at the screen.

**Jessa Vale**  
Exactly.

`twinkle.puss` clocks that hit and leans in.

**twinkle.puss**  
Our approach is to stabilize the narrative and the workflow at the same time. Otherwise the org just re-enacts its anxiety in a more expensive interface.

`ariane.emory` glances at him: that was good.

**Jessa Vale**  
Can you do a two-week sprint? Intake, interviews, maybe a pilot recommendation for support automation?

Silence in the trio boxes. Then `twinkle.puss` smiles.

**twinkle.puss**  
Yes.

**ariane.emory**  
Subject to scope alignment, yes.

**bl0ng**  
We'd want to look at current human override pathways.

**Jessa Vale**  
Great. Send me a proposal today. If it looks sane, we move.

The call ends.

Nobody breathes.

**twinkle.puss**  
That was erotic.

**ariane.emory**  
That was binding.

**bl0ng**  
I think we all mean the same thing.

### SCENE 9

**INT. APARTMENT - CONTINUOUS**

The adrenaline surge is ugly and pure.

**twinkle.puss**  
Okay. We need pricing that communicates confidence but not indictment.

**ariane.emory**  
We need scope, timeline, deliverables, and language that does not imply we can heal a company.

**bl0ng**  
I can write a matrix that looks expensive.

**twinkle.puss**  
You just described consulting.

### SCENE 10

**INT. BACKCHANNEL / PROPOSAL DOC - DAY**

They build the proposal in real time.

Section headers appear:

- `Current State Diagnosis`
- `Cross-Functional Risk Mapping`
- `Human-in-the-Loop Pilot Design`
- `Executive Readout`

**ariane.emory**  
Those are all defensible.

**bl0ng**  
For avoidance of doubt, defensible is not synonymous with true.

**ariane.emory**  
Today it is.

`twinkle.puss` drags in a slide with the line: `Adoption fails where language and authority drift apart.`

**bl0ng**  
God. We deserve to get paid for this.

### SCENE 11

**INT. KITCHEN - LATE AFTERNOON**

Everyone is fading.

`twinkle.puss` stares at a pricing cell. His joke velocity drops a half beat, which is how this show does seriousness.

**twinkle.puss**  
What number makes us sound real on purpose.

`ariane.emory` looks up.

**ariane.emory**  
What number covers the labor.

**twinkle.puss**  
No, I know. I just mean... what number makes it not feel like we're asking for lunch money.

`bl0ng` pretends to keep typing, giving him privacy by not helping.

**ariane.emory**  
Eight thousand for two weeks.

**twinkle.puss**  
That's either adulthood or criminal self-esteem.

**ariane.emory**  
Those are often adjacent.

Beat.

**twinkle.puss**  
I need this to be real by tomorrow.

The line lands inside the pricing conversation like a dropped plate.

Then `twinkle.puss` grins too hard.

**twinkle.puss**  
Not emotionally. Administratively.

`ariane.emory` just nods once and keeps working.

### ACT THREE

### SCENE 12

**INT. ARIANE.EMORY'S APARTMENT - EVENING**

The proposal is almost done.

`bl0ng` adds a footer: `Cataclysm Consulting - Confidential`.

**ariane.emory**  
We have no authority to call anything confidential.

**bl0ng**  
It helps the PDF believe in itself.

**twinkle.puss**  
Leave it. It's couture.

`ariane.emory` rubs her eyes, then opens a new doc: `follow-up-email`.

**ariane.emory**  
We send plain language in the body. No seduction. No apocalypse words. No using the phrase `holding environment`.

**twinkle.puss**  
You're strangling our culture in infancy.

### SCENE 13

**INT. BACKCHANNEL - NIGHT**

We watch the email being composed.

`Jessa - attaching a brief proposal for a two-week sprint focused on current-state diagnosis, pilot design, and cross-functional alignment. happy to discuss revisions if useful.`

**twinkle.puss**  
Needs one drop of sauce.

He adds: `We think speed matters here, but not at the expense of traceability.`

`ariane.emory` considers deleting it, then leaves it.

**ariane.emory**  
Send.

`twinkle.puss` freezes.

**bl0ng**  
Now who's doing ritual around the button.

**twinkle.puss**  
Shut up. This one matters.

He sends.

Three of them stare at the screen like it might blink first.

Notification.

`Jessa Vale`: `Looks good. Need vendor setup + agreement. Can we start Monday if legal doesn't scream?`

`twinkle.puss` puts both hands over his face and laughs like he's being robbed.

**twinkle.puss**  
We're so back for a thing that was never previously forward.

**bl0ng**  
I would like everyone to note that the market has validated my worst traits.

`ariane.emory` is smiling despite herself. She hates that the smile is real.

### TAG

**INT. ARIANE.EMORY'S APARTMENT - LATER**

The room has gone slightly quiet. Victory is now administrative.

An email arrives from `procurement@vale.ai`.

Subject: `Vendor Intake + MSA Required`.

Attached: seven documents.

`twinkle.puss` reads the filenames like they are curses.

**twinkle.puss**  
What's an MSA.

**ariane.emory**  
Tomorrow's problem.

**bl0ng**  
No. A bigger, more elegantly formatted version of tonight's problem.

On Backchannel, `HexadecimalBoy` posts in the company server:

`we out here`

The trio look at it.

Cut to black.
