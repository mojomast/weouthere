## Critical Path

The schedule's critical path is:

1. Canon and development foundation complete.
2. Episode outlines and six scripts reach finance-ready draft state.
3. Budget, cashflow, and investor materials finalize.
4. Financing close and legal entity/insurance readiness complete.
5. Casting, crewing, location lock, and shooting schedule lock complete.
6. Principal photography occurs within Q3 2026.
7. Editorial, VFX, sound, color, and QC complete without major pickup cycle.
8. Final masters and release materials deliver by Q1-Q2 2027.

Any slip longer than two weeks in steps 2, 4, 5, 6, or 7 threatens the Q1-Q2 2027 delivery target.

## Slack / Buffer Assumptions

| Buffer | Amount | Held In | Use Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Script emergency buffer** | 2 weeks | June-July 2026 | Used only for draft delays or essential rewrites |
| **Prep buffer** | 1 week | Late August 2026 | Protects casting, permitting, and tech scout slippage |
| **Production recovery buffer** | 2 shoot days equivalent | Inside September 2026 plan | Reserved for weather, overages, or technical resets |
| **Post buffer** | 2-3 weeks | January-February 2027 | Covers VFX, mix, QC, and caption delays |
| **Launch buffer** | 2-4 weeks | March-April 2027 | Covers buyer notes, festival response, or release-date optimization |

The schedule assumes buffers are consumed in order of least market impact: internal rewrite time first, then prep float, then post float, with launch window movement as the last resort.

## Schedule Risks That Threaten Delivery

| Risk | Pressure Point | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Script delays** | Pushes financing close and prep start | Enforce June first-draft deadline and use script buffer early |
| **Financing gap** | Stops crewing and location commitments | Run investor outreach in parallel, maintain scalable budget tiers |
| **Hybrid pipeline failure** | Slows prep, edit, and VFX | Prototype screen capture and post workflow before cameras |
| **Casting delays** | Compresses rehearsal and schedule lock | Begin outreach before full financing close where legally feasible |
| **Post overrun** | Threatens Q1-Q2 2027 delivery | Lock pickups early, cap VFX scope, protect final mix and QC dates |

## Operating Notes

- This is a master schedule, not a day-out-of-days or stripboard.
- Month-level targets should be translated into weekly execution plans by each owner once downstream docs are created.
- If Gate C slips past July 2026, the Q3 2026 production target is immediately at risk and requires replanning.
- If picture lock slips beyond January 2027, the schedule should default to Window B or Window C rather than compress final QC.
