## Outreach Use Rules
- customize the first two sentences for the actual recipient
- keep the body short enough to read on a phone
- lead with fit, not with biography or process
- include one private link bundle only: deck, teaser, or screener as appropriate
- close with a clear next ask: watch, meet, or consider for program/release fit

## Customization Notes
- for premium buyers, emphasize prestige and critical-discourse upside
- for festivals, emphasize authorship and formal execution
- for digital partners, emphasize controlled release and online-native discoverability
- for international partners, reduce US-industry shorthand and foreground universal work-pressure themes
