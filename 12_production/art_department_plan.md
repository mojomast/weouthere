## Department Objective

The art department must make the show feel specific, contemporary, and psychologically loaded without building a large-world premium-cable footprint. The world should feel lived in, improvised, and over-signified by people using aesthetics as self-defense.

## Visual Environment Rules

- near-present, not sci-fi
- messy competence over glossy futurism
- tools, packaging, furniture, cables, whiteboards, stickers, dead plants, and improvised storage should tell story
- every recurring space should show work, aspiration, and depletion at once
- environments should evolve across the season as instability accumulates

## Standing Environment Plan

### Set A: Cataclysm Base

Required zones:

- main workstation cluster
- video-call capable corner
- whiteboard / planning wall
- improvised presentation zone
- spill surface for props, paperwork, snacks, chargers, and chaos residue

Build notes:

- needs flexible wall treatment for lensing variation
- must support practical monitors without constant reflection failure
- should reset quickly between episodes and emotional states

### Set B: Personal / Domestic Spill Space

Required zones:

- bed or sofa work zone
- desk or vanity work zone
- doorway / threshold playing area
- surfaces for wardrobe and props continuity

Design goal:

- the room should reflect aspirational identity losing ground to actual life management

### Set C: Utility Connector

Required zones:

- hallway or corridor stretch
- entry threshold
- practical corner for calls, arguments, and pickups

Value:

- low build complexity, high schedule rescue value

## Seasonal Degradation Plan

The season arc should register in the environments.

| Season Phase | Art Shift |
| --- | --- |
| Mania | energetic surfaces, overprepared optimism, too many ideas visible |
| Fragile success | increased branding, pseudo-professional order, cleaner presentation |
| Ethical drift | denser paperwork, workaround objects, hidden mess |
| Containment failure | collisions between order and panic become visible |
| Ironic collapse | broken systems, depleted consumables, uglier workarounds |
| Exposure to consequences | evidence remains, attempted cleanup feels incomplete |

## Practical Locations: Art Strategy

- practicals should be enhanced, not overbuilt
- bring a portable kit for tech-world dressing: chargers, laptop stands, notebooks, binders, branded printouts, empty shipping materials, office snacks, temporary signage
- plan light-touch redress packages so one office can cover multiple clients or institutions

## Department Deliverables

### Before Camera Tests

- look reference board
- standing set priority list
- scenic / dressing budget split
- hero graphics inventory with ownership notes

### Before Tech Scout

- floor plans or rough blocking layouts for standing sets
- monitor placement and glare notes
- redress matrix for multipurpose locations

### Before Shoot Week 1

- final set dressing bible for recurring spaces
- reset photo system
- continuity tagging system for recurring art assets
- strike and storage plan for graphics-heavy surfaces

## Collaboration With Graphics / Props / Post

### Graphics

- all in-world logos, decks, printed matter, labels, and signage must route through one graphics approval chain
- art department owns physical output and placement
- post owns replacements or extensions only when clearly assigned

### Props

- physical paperwork and tech clutter must be coordinated, not duplicated between props and set dressing
- assign ownership for every recurring hero object

### Post

- photograph all monitor setups and practical graphics placements
- deliver source files for any graphic likely to reappear or require patching

## Budget Priorities

Spend first on:

- recurring set control
- graphics packages with repeat use
- surfaces and dressing that sell the world in medium shot

Spend later on:

- one-off decorative flourishes
- highly custom scenic details with low screen time

## Art Department Risks

- UI and print graphics developed too late to test practically
- art and props duplicating purchases
- locations that forbid adequate dressing
- set evolution not tracked, causing continuity resets to drift
