# 11_legal/writer_agreement_template.md

## Purpose

Provide a practical writer agreement template for WE OUT HERE covering commissioned writing and assignment of rights for scripts, outlines, rewrites, punch-up, and related development materials. This is a starting template for attorney adaptation, not final legal advice.

## Owner/Subagent
legal_agent

## Status
Draft v1 - internal template. Not approved for signature as-is. Counsel should adapt this form for compensation structure, guild issues, credit, work-made-for-hire treatment, reserved rights, moral rights handling, and dispute terms.

## Dependencies

- Correct company legal name
- Scope of the writer's services and deliverables
- Compensation terms and payment schedule
- Credit expectations
- Any WGA or other guild analysis
- Attorney review before use

## Plain-Language Notes

- Use this when someone is writing material for WE OUT HERE.
- Payment terms and credit are important, but the non-negotiable business point is that the company needs clear rights to use the material.
- If a writer contributed before papering the deal, get a retroactive confirmation and assignment reviewed by counsel.
- If any union or guild rules may apply, do not rely on this template without specialized review.

## Attorney Review Flags

- Whether the writer should be treated as an employee, loan-out, or independent contractor.
- Whether work-made-for-hire language is valid for the specific deliverables and jurisdiction.
- Whether the credit clause, separated rights, residuals, or guild requirements need major changes.
- Whether waiver of moral rights, injunctive relief limits, and arbitration language are appropriate.

## Template

**WRITER AGREEMENT**

This Writer Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of `[DATE]` by and between `[COMPANY LEGAL NAME]`, with an address at `[ADDRESS]` ("Company"), and `[WRITER LEGAL NAME]`, with an address at `[ADDRESS]` ("Writer").

### 1. Project

The services relate to the original scripted sitcom project currently titled **WE OUT HERE** (the "Project"). The Project may include a pilot, additional episodes, a series bible, outlines, character materials, promotional materials, and other related development assets.

### 2. Engagement

Company engages Writer to render writing and related creative services for the Project, and Writer accepts that engagement, subject to the terms of this Agreement.

### 3. Services And Deliverables

Writer will provide the following services and deliverables:

- `[pilot draft / rewrite / polish / series bible / episode outlines / punch-up / consultation]`
- Delivery schedule: `[DATES OR MILESTONES]`
- Format and submission method: `[PDF / Final Draft / shared drive / other]`

Writer will perform the services in a professional and timely manner and will reasonably collaborate with Company regarding revisions within the agreed scope.

### 4. Compensation

As full consideration for the rights granted and services rendered, Company will pay Writer:

- Fee: `[AMOUNT]`
- Payment schedule: `[on signing / on commencement / on delivery / on acceptance / milestone schedule]`
- Reimbursable pre-approved expenses, if any: `[DETAILS OR NONE]`

Unless expressly stated otherwise, Writer is not entitled to additional compensation, royalties, residuals, backend, or bonus payments.

### 5. Work Made For Hire / Assignment

To the maximum extent permitted by law, all results and proceeds of Writer's services, including all drafts, rewrites, outlines, notes, characters, dialogue, jokes, concepts, and other materials created, prepared, submitted, or contributed by Writer for the Project (collectively, the "Materials") are specially ordered or commissioned by Company as works made for hire for Company.

To the extent any of the Materials do not qualify as works made for hire, Writer hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Company, exclusively and throughout the universe in perpetuity, all right, title, and interest in and to the Materials and all copyrights and renewals and extensions thereof, together with all allied, ancillary, adaptation, sequel, remake, spin-off, merchandising, promotion, and exploitation rights of every kind now known or later devised.

### 6. Further Assurances

Writer will execute and deliver such additional documents as Company may reasonably request to confirm, evidence, or perfect Company's rights in the Materials. If Writer fails to do so after reasonable request, Company is appointed Writer's attorney-in-fact, coupled with an interest, solely for that limited purpose, to execute such confirmatory documents where legally permissible.

### 7. Reserved Rights

Except for rights expressly granted to Writer in this Agreement, if any, Writer retains no ownership interest in the Materials or the Project. Any portfolio or excerpt use by Writer requires Company approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld after public release, subject to confidentiality, spoilers, and distributor restrictions.

### 8. Credit

If Company uses the Materials, Writer shall receive credit, if any, in the form `[CREDIT FORMULATION OR SUBJECT TO COMPANY DISCRETION / GUILD RULES]`, subject to Company's final determination, technical limitations, distributor requirements, and any applicable guild obligations.

No casual or preliminary failure to accord credit will be deemed a breach if Company uses reasonable efforts to correct prospectively where practical. Writer's sole remedy for credit breach, if any, shall be money damages, and not injunctive relief, to the extent permitted by law.

### 9. Originality And Clearances

Writer represents and warrants that:

1. the Materials will be original to Writer except for material supplied or approved by Company;
2. Writer has the full right to enter into this Agreement and grant the rights granted hereunder;
3. the Materials will not knowingly infringe any copyright, trademark, right of publicity, privacy right, or other proprietary right of any third party;
4. the Materials will not knowingly defame any identifiable person; and
5. Writer will not intentionally include unlicensed lyrics, substantial quotes, screenshots, or other third-party protected materials unless specifically requested in writing by Company for later clearance review.

Special Project note:

- If Writer depicts an online community or chat platform, Writer will use fictional naming and treatment unless Company specifically requests a reviewed real-world reference.
- Writer should avoid scripts that depend on literal Discord branding, logos, UI imitation, or sponsor-like implications.

### 10. Confidentiality

Writer will keep non-public information regarding the Project, Company, its business plans, materials, budgets, cast, crew, financing, and distribution arrangements strictly confidential and will not disclose or exploit such information except as necessary to perform services under this Agreement.

### 11. Publicity

Writer will not announce Writer's involvement or release excerpts, drafts, pages, or behind-the-scenes information without Company's prior written approval.

### 12. Independent Contractor / Taxes

Unless otherwise required by law or stated in writing, Writer is engaged as an independent contractor and not as an employee, partner, or joint venturer of Company. Writer is responsible for Writer's own taxes, insurance, and withholdings arising from amounts paid under this Agreement.

### 13. Indemnity

Writer will indemnify and hold harmless Company and its successors, licensees, distributors, and assigns from third-party claims arising out of a material breach of Writer's representations, warranties, or obligations under this Agreement, subject to applicable law and ordinary notice-and-control-of-defense provisions.

### 14. Remedies

Company may seek all remedies available at law or in equity for breach of this Agreement, provided that Writer's remedies for any alleged breach by Company shall be limited to money damages, and Writer shall not seek to enjoin or restrain the development, production, distribution, exhibition, advertising, or exploitation of the Project, to the extent permitted by law.

This Agreement contains the entire understanding between the parties concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes prior discussions on that subject. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both parties.
