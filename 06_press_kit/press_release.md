### `WE OUT HERE` Launches Press and Partner Outreach for Sharp New AI-Era Dark Comedy Series

`WE OUT HERE`, a `6 x 30 min single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy`, is launching press, festival, and partner outreach with a full project materials package for buyers, collaborators, and cultural press.

The series follows `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory`, three burned-out technologists who launch the chaotic AI consultancy `Cataclysm Consulting` from the fictional platform `Backchannel`. What begins as a semi-ironic hustle for survival turns into a manic attempt to manufacture legitimacy through decks, disclaimers, pseudo-process, and vibes. As the company gains traction, the trio's friendship becomes the failed holding environment that keeps the business alive while making everyone less stable.

Smart, darkly funny, emotionally grounded, and internet-literate, `WE OUT HERE` is designed as a formally distinctive but production-manageable half-hour series combining desktop realism, live action, and surreal insert sequences. Rather than treating AI as abstract futurism, the project focuses on the present-tense social reality around it: panic, performance, labor anxiety, pseudo-expertise, and the monetization of uncertainty.

"`WE OUT HERE` is about what happens when highly articulate, psychologically frayed adults discover that the market will reward confidence long before it verifies competence," the creative team said in a prepared statement. "It is a comedy about language doing too much work: at work, in friendship, in self-justification, and in the fantasy that if you can frame something well enough, you do not have to contain it."

The six-episode first season follows a precise escalation from `mania` to `fragile success`, `ethical drift`, `containment failure`, `ironic collapse`, and finally `exposure to consequences`. The episode lineup is:

1. `"Don't Send That"`
2. `"Terms of Abuse"`
3. `"The Manic Defense"`
4. `"Contain Me Daddy"`
5. `"The Superego Collapse"`
6. `"Let Him Cook()"`

The project is aimed at audiences looking for contemporary prestige comedy with behavioral specificity, cultural accuracy, and a strong emotional engine beneath the satire. Its core audience includes digitally fluent adults, prestige-comedy viewers, and industry-facing audiences who recognize the changing language of work, legitimacy, and online performance.

Press materials now available include a series overview, creator and producer statements, public episode guide, character sheet, festival synopses, and approved Q&A for media and partner conversations.

### About `WE OUT HERE`

`WE OUT HERE` is a `6 x 30 min single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy` about three burned-out technologists who start an AI consultancy from `Backchannel`, a fictional group-chat platform, and build a business powerful enough to expose the instability holding it together. The series combines desktop realism, live action, and surreal insert sequences to tell a smart, darkly funny, emotionally grounded story about work, self-invention, and consequences in the AI era.
