## Content

### Short Synopsis
Three burned-out technologists launch a chaotic AI consultancy from the fictional platform `Backchannel` and become just successful enough to expose the instability, fraudulence, and friendship damage holding the company together.

### Medium Synopsis
`WE OUT HERE` is a `6 x 30 min single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy` about `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory`, three burned-out technologists who build `Cataclysm Consulting`, an improvised AI consultancy born in a group chat on `Backchannel`. What starts as a semi-ironic hustle for survival turns into a real business powered by pitch decks, pseudo-legal language, unstable judgment, and mutual enabling. As the company gains legitimacy, the trio's intelligence, charm, and fluency become the very forces driving them toward ethical drift and public collapse.

### Long Synopsis
`WE OUT HERE` is a smart, darkly funny, emotionally grounded half-hour series set in a near-present world where AI has made expertise newly theatrical. The story follows `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory`, three burned-out technologists who launch `Cataclysm Consulting` from `Backchannel`, a fictional group-chat platform for work, friendship, and private spirals. Their consultancy begins as a joke, then a hustle, then an actual company, largely because the culture around them is irrational enough to reward confidence before it verifies competence.

Across six episodes, the series tracks a clear escalation from mania to fragile success, ethical drift, containment failure, ironic collapse, and exposure to consequences. The formal language combines desktop realism, live action, and surreal insert sequences to capture how contemporary work is shaped by chats, decks, DMs, legal hedging, self-mythology, and the unbearable permanence of sending. The comedy is precise and behavior-driven, but the emotional core is a friendship system that functions as management, therapy, witness, and mutual destruction at the same time.

Rather than offering broad startup satire or abstract futurism, `WE OUT HERE` focuses on the human systems around AI: labor anxiety, pseudo-expertise, performative certainty, and the fantasy that language can keep reality from arriving. It is a series about smart people who know exactly what they are doing wrong, and keep doing it because it works just well enough.
