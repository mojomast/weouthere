### Project Snapshot
- Title: `WE OUT HERE`
- Format: `6 x 30 min single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy`
- Production style: `desktop realism + live action + surreal insert sequences`
- Series premise: Three burned-out technologists launch chaotic AI consultancy `Cataclysm Consulting` from the fictional platform `Backchannel` and discover that the same fluency making them marketable also makes them dangerously uncontained.
- Tone: smart, darkly funny, emotionally grounded, internet-literate
- Status: packaging and partner outreach materials in active circulation

### Short Description
`WE OUT HERE` is a sharp, contemporary half-hour about work, performance, friendship, and self-delusion under AI-era pressure. The series follows `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory` as they turn a group-chat joke into `Cataclysm Consulting`, a boutique AI consultancy built on speed, rhetoric, unstable judgment, and the market value of sounding certain before anyone has verified anything.

Set in a near-present professional culture where expertise has become theatrical, the show blends readable desktop storytelling with grounded live-action scenes and controlled surreal inserts. The comedy comes from psychological precision rather than broad parody: these characters are funny because they are bright, overclocked, self-aware, and still unable to stop themselves.

### Why The Project Is Press- and Festival-Relevant
- It captures a current cultural condition without reducing itself to an issue piece.
- It treats AI as a human systems problem: status, labor anxiety, fraudulence, self-narration, and consequence.
- It offers a formally distinctive but producible screen language.
- It delivers three standout lead roles with clear comic and dramatic range.
- It is built as a compact six-episode season with a precise escalation spine.

### Core Themes
- Burnout monetized as expertise
- Friendship functioning as infrastructure
- The gap between fluency and legitimacy
- Platform-shaped communication as plot, workplace, and evidence trail
- Contemporary work as performance, improvisation, and moral drift

### Season Arc
Season 1 escalates in a locked progression:
1. `Mania`
2. `Fragile success`
3. `Ethical drift`
4. `Containment failure`
5. `Ironic collapse`
6. `Exposure to consequences`

Episode titles:
1. `"Don't Send That"`
2. `"Terms of Abuse"`
3. `"The Manic Defense"`
4. `"Contain Me Daddy"`
5. `"The Superego Collapse"`
6. `"Let Him Cook()"`

### Key Characters

#### `twinkle.puss`
The impulsive visionary: charismatic, persuasive, and always one escalation away from turning shame into momentum and momentum into liability.

#### `bl0ng`
The failed superego: technically brilliant, rhetorically armored, and expert at building just enough process to let bad decisions keep moving.

#### `ariane.emory`
The detached meta-observer: emotionally acute, structurally competent, and trapped by the belief that if she can contain the damage she can also survive it.
