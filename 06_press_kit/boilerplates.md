### Series Boilerplate - 100 Words
`WE OUT HERE` is a `6 x 30 min single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy` following `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory`, three burned-out technologists who build `Cataclysm Consulting`, an improvised AI consultancy launched from `Backchannel`, a fictional group-chat platform. Smart, darkly funny, emotionally grounded, and internet-literate, `WE OUT HERE` is driven by a friendship that functions as management, witness, and mutual destruction all at once.

- Always describe format as `6 x 30 min single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy`.
- Always refer to `Backchannel` as a fictional platform.
- Use `Cataclysm Consulting` as the working company name unless updated by canon lock.
- Avoid calling the series sci-fi, mockumentary, or broad startup parody.
