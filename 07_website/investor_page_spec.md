# 07_website/investor_page_spec.md

## Purpose

Specify the public-facing Investors / Partners page for **WE OUT HERE**, including positioning, disclosure boundaries, and inquiry flow.

## Owner/Subagent

bizdev_agent

## Status

Draft v1 - partner-ready overview spec

## Dependencies

- `07_website/website_copy.md`
- Approved investor-safe messaging
- Legal review of financing and partnership language
- Contact routing for qualified conversations

## Content

## Page Goal

Give aligned investors, strategic partners, and senior collaborators enough information to take the project seriously and request a conversation, without publishing sensitive materials or overpromising.

## Audience

- Angel or private backers
- Strategic partners
- Executive producers or producing partners
- Brand, distribution, or institutional collaborators where relevant

## Required Sections

### 1. Page Hero

- H1: `For Investors / Partners`
- Intro paragraph
- CTA: `Request a Conversation`

### 2. Project Snapshot

Fields:

- Title
- Format
- Genre
- Stage
- Creator(s)
- Location / production base
- Current materials status

### 3. Why This Project

Short editorial section covering:

- Cultural relevance
- Distinct tonal position
- Why the audience and moment are real

### 4. Why This Team

Contents:

- Creator / producer bios
- Proof of seriousness
- Optional selected prior work or affiliations

### 5. Partnership Modes

Possible categories:

- Financing conversation
- Strategic production support
- Distribution / festival partnership
- Brand or institutional collaboration only if genuinely relevant

### 6. Materials Boundary Note

Explicitly state that fuller materials are available selectively upon qualified inquiry.

### 7. Inquiry Form Or Contact Block

Required fields:

- Name
- Email
- Organization
- Interest area
- Short note

## Tone Requirements

- Confident
- Controlled
- Specific
- Not breathless

## Disclosure Rules

- Do not publish budget details unless explicitly approved.
- Do not imply secured attachments or financing that is not real.
- Do not publish confidential deck content verbatim.
- Avoid exaggerated language about market inevitability.

## Trust Signals

- Clear project facts
- Cohesive public materials
- Professional inquiry path
- Links to Press Kit and About page

## Functional Requirements

- Form routing for partner inquiries
- CRM or inbox tagging if available
- Ability to update stage and materials status quickly

## Analytics

- CTA clicks
- Form starts
- Form completions
- Cross-navigation to Press and About pages

## Success Criteria

- Serious visitors understand the project fast.
- The page feels legitimate, not speculative.
- Qualified conversations increase without creating public overexposure.
