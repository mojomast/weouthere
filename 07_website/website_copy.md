## About The Series Copy

### Page Intro

WE OUT HERE is a premium indie dark sitcom built around the emotional logic of startup culture, internet self-construction, and the performance demands of modern creative life. The series tracks a tightly wound social world where work, friendship, ambition, romance, and identity management can no longer be separated cleanly.

### Premise Section

Everyone in this world is trying to become undeniable. Some want money. Some want relevance. Some want cultural legitimacy. Some just want to stop feeling replaceable. The problem is that the same systems promising transcendence keep producing insecurity, self-surveillance, and weird new forms of intimacy. WE OUT HERE turns that pressure into comedy without letting anyone off the hook.

### Tone Section

The show lives in the overlap between psychologically sharp character comedy, startup social realism, and internet-era dread. It is funny because the characters are serious. It is serious because they cannot stop performing.

### Themes Section

- Ambition as identity system
- Work replacing personality
- Friendship under competition pressure
- Online visibility and self-erasure
- Belonging, status, and emotional distortion

## Copy Rules

- Spell the title as `WE OUT HERE` in all caps in headlines and metadata.
- Do not call the show a startup satire in generic terms.
- Avoid empty prestige phrases like `cinematic storytelling`, `bold voices`, or `disrupting entertainment`.
- Keep status language factual and dateable.
- Reuse the same approved quick facts everywhere.
