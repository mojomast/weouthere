## Content

## Page Goal

Answer repeated public questions clearly, reduce avoidable inbound, and set expectations around access, submissions, and project status.

## Question Groups

### 1. About The Project

Recommended questions:

- What is WE OUT HERE?
- What format is the series?
- What stage is the project currently in?
- Where can I read more about the series?

### 2. Press And Materials

- Where can I find the press kit?
- Who should I contact for press or festival inquiries?
- Are screeners available?

### 3. Casting

- Are you currently casting?
- Do you accept unsolicited submissions?
- Where should agents or talent submit materials?

### 4. Jobs / Production

- Are there any open production roles?
- Do you accept general-interest applications?
- Are roles paid?

### 5. Community

- How do I join the Discord or community?
- What kind of community is this?
- What happens if the community is not open yet?

### 6. Contact And Updates

- How do I contact the team?
- How do I get updates?
- Do you reply to every message?

## UX Requirements

- Group questions by topic.
- Support deep-linking to individual questions if practical.
- Default to collapsed accordions on mobile.
- Include contextual links to Contact, Press, Casting, Jobs, and Community pages.

## Tone Requirements

- Direct and factual
- Calm, not defensive
- Short enough to scan

## Maintenance Rules

- FAQ answers should reuse canonical facts from other pages.
- Review after every major status change.
- Remove stale references to open calls or deadlines.
- Avoid using FAQ as a dumping ground for information that belongs on a primary page.

## Schema Notes

- Use FAQPage structured data only for questions that appear visibly on the page and reflect current truth.

## Success Criteria

- Repetitive inbound drops.
- Users self-serve common answers.
- FAQ remains current and credible.
