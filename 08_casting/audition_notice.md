## Audition Notice
Project: `WE OUT HERE`

Format: 6 x 30 minute single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy

Company: `Cataclysm Consulting`

Casting approach: Open casting, performance-first

Tone: Smart, darkly funny, psychologically sharp, internet-native, humane

## Project Description
`WE OUT HERE` follows `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory`, three burned-out technologists who launch a chaotic AI consultancy from `Backchannel` and discover that the friendship structure keeping the company alive is also making everyone less stable.

The show combines desktop realism, live action, and surreal inserts. We are looking for actors who can handle contemporary language, precise subtext, dark comedy, and emotionally grounded instability.

## Roles Currently Reading
- Series Regular: `twinkle.puss`
- Series Regular: `bl0ng`
- Series Regular: `ariane.emory`
- Select recurring roles
- Rolling consideration for day players

## What We Are Looking For
- Strong acting craft first
- Comfort with fast, language-driven material
- Ability to play intelligence, contradiction, and need
- Tonal agility across comedy and drama
- Real listening and behavioral specificity on camera

## Audition Materials
Actors will receive:
- role breakdown
- sides
- self-tape instructions
- callback or chemistry-read details if advanced

## Submission Materials Requested
- Headshot
- Resume
- Reel, if available
- Contact and representation information
- Any relevant scheduling conflicts

## Important Note To Actors And Reps
This is a performance-first process. We welcome submissions from represented and unrepresented actors where appropriate through official casting channels. We are interested in discovery as well as established talent. Please focus on specificity, behavior, and clarity rather than trying to signal "tech world" in a broad way.

## Callback Note
Select actors will be invited to callbacks and chemistry reads. For leads, chemistry will be a significant part of final decision-making.
