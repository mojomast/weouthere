## Series Overview
`WE OUT HERE` is a 6 x 30 minute single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy about three burned-out technologists who launch `Cataclysm Consulting`, a chaotic AI consultancy born inside `Backchannel`. The tone is smart, darkly funny, internet-native, and humane. Open casting. Performance-first.

## Series Regular Roles
### twinkle.puss
Series Regular

Character summary:
Late 20s to mid 30s. Magnetic, volatile, fast-thinking, socially dangerous, hilarious, and frighteningly persuasive. `twinkle.puss` can turn humiliation into momentum and panic into brand language in real time. He is the engine behind `Cataclysm Consulting`: closer, instigator, myth-maker, and walking liability.

Performance requirements:
- Exceptional comic timing with real dramatic depth
- Can pivot from seduction to panic to command within a beat
- Strong command of dense, verbal material
- Must project intelligence, appetite, shame sensitivity, and force of will
- Needs to feel like someone other people would follow even when they know better

Essence notes:
This is not a "chaotic fun girl" part. The actor must understand the difference between charisma and dissociation, and make both playable at once. The role needs glamour, danger, speed, and emotional cost.

Audition note:
We are looking for actors who can make the language feel spontaneous, tactical, and alive rather than performed for cleverness.

### bl0ng
Series Regular

Character summary:
Late 20s to late 30s. Dry, hyper-articulate, technically gifted, avoidant, funny, and chronically defended. `bl0ng` is the systems brain of `Cataclysm Consulting`, forever turning dread into theory and bad ideas into workflows. He often sees the danger first and still helps build the machine.

Performance requirements:
- Strong deadpan and precision with complex language
- Can land humor through thought process rather than punchlines
- Must reveal anxiety, ego, and loneliness beneath control
- Comfortable with fast dialogue, technical framing, and sudden emotional exposure
- Needs authority without conventional sturdiness

Essence notes:
This is not a stock coder or alt-comedy snarker. The actor should feel like someone who uses brilliance to stay one step removed from ordinary human vulnerability.

Audition note:
We are looking for actors who can make abstraction funny, specific, and behaviorally true.

### ariane.emory
Series Regular

Character summary:
Late 20s to late 30s. Precise, composed, emotionally acute, overfunctioning, and very funny in a dry, surgical register. `ariane.emory` is the closest thing the group has to a container. She can see what is wrong before anyone else, but insight does not spare her from attachment, ambition, or complicity.

Performance requirements:
- Strong stillness and camera presence
- Dry comic intelligence with layered emotional stakes
- Can play competence, resentment, care, and exhaustion simultaneously
- Must handle sharp dialogue and quieter internal beats equally well
- Needs to feel deeply contemporary and fully human, never merely "the responsible one"

Essence notes:
This role lives in the gap between moral clarity and behavioral entanglement. The actor should be able to make restraint feel active, funny, and costly.

Audition note:
We are looking for actors who can play subtext, containment, and late-breaking honesty without flattening the role into seriousness.

## Casting Notes For Reps
- All roles are open to discovery and established talent
- Strong interest in actors with theater rigor, indie screen naturalism, and/or sharp dark-comedy chops
- Internet fluency is welcome, but craft is the key determinant
- Chemistry, listening, and tonal control matter as much as individual heat

## Submission Guidance
Please submit actors who can handle smart language, tonal volatility, and psychological complexity. Standard headshot, resume, reel, and any relevant contemporary comedy/drama footage are helpful. Self-tape instructions will be provided with the audition notice.
