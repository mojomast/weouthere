## Master Casting Call Copy
Casting is underway for `WE OUT HERE`, a 6 x 30 minute single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy from `Cataclysm Consulting`.

We are seeking smart, specific, emotionally precise actors for a performance-first, open casting process. The show follows `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory`, three burned-out technologists who launch a chaotic AI consultancy from `Backchannel` and ride a manic wave of sudden legitimacy into ethical drift, containment failure, and public collapse.

Tone: smart, darkly funny, psychologically sharp, internet-native, and humane.

We are especially interested in actors who can handle fast language, tonal pivots, behavioral specificity, and dark comedy rooted in real emotional stakes.

Series regular, recurring, and day-player submissions welcome through appropriate casting channels.
