## Canonical Synopsis Copy

### Short Synopsis
Three burned-out technologists launch a chaotic AI consultancy from `Backchannel` and become just successful enough to destroy themselves.

### Medium Synopsis
`WE OUT HERE` is an indie prestige dark sitcom about three internet-native founders who turn shared burnout into `Cataclysm Consulting`, an AI consultancy built on speed, decks, disclaimers, and the market value of sounding certain. As the company gains traction, their friendship becomes the failed holding environment keeping the business alive while making everyone less stable.

### Canon Logline
Three burned-out technologists launch a chaotic AI consultancy from `Backchannel` and try to bootstrap legitimacy through manic hustle, irony, pseudo-legal improvisation, and memes. Their friendship becomes the failed holding environment that keeps the company alive while making everyone less stable.

Use the canon logline in high-trust materials. Use the short or medium synopsis for posters and public-facing campaign art where space is limited.

## Press And Festival Boilerplate
`WE OUT HERE` is a 6 x 30 minute single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy. The series follows `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, and `ariane.emory`, three burned-out technologists who launch `Cataclysm Consulting`, a chaotic AI consultancy assembled inside `Backchannel`. Told through desktop realism, live action, and surreal inserts, the series tracks their rise from manic invention to ethical drift, containment failure, and exposure to consequences.
