## Canon Lock
- Title: `WE OUT HERE`
- Format: `6 x 30 min single-camera dark sitcom / tech psychocomedy`
- Style: `desktop realism + live action + surreal inserts`
- Leads: `twinkle.puss`, `bl0ng`, `ariane.emory`
- Company: `Cataclysm Consulting`

## Primary Logline
Three burned-out technologists launch a chaotic AI consultancy from Backchannel and ride a manic wave of sudden success into ethical drift, containment failure, and public collapse.

## Secondary Logline Options

### Variant 1: Clean Industry Version
When three emotionally overclocked technologists turn a private group-chat bit into a real AI consultancy, their hustle, friendship, and moral judgment unravel at the exact speed their company takes off.

### Variant 2: Character-Forward Version
`WE OUT HERE` follows twinkle.puss, bl0ng, and ariane.emory, three brilliant, burned-out operators who build Cataclysm Consulting out of Backchannel and discover that the same chemistry making them funny, sharp, and marketable also makes them catastrophically unsafe.

### Variant 3: Tonal Version
Part workplace comedy, part psychological spiral, `WE OUT HERE` tracks three online-native founders who use AI hype, ironic confidence, and shared delusion to bootstrap a consultancy that becomes just successful enough to destroy them.

### Variant 4: Marketplace Version
In a near-present tech economy where nobody wants to admit they do not understand AI, three desperate consultants monetize the confusion until their fake-it-till-you-make-it operation turns into an actual ethical disaster.

### Variant 5: Short Form Version
Three burned-out technologists start an AI consultancy in a group chat and accidentally manifest the exact kind of company that should never exist.

### Variant 6: Voice-Driven Version
Three internet-poisoned technologists launch Cataclysm Consulting from Backchannel, where every joke is a strategy, every strategy is a coping mechanism, and every win makes the coming collapse harder to stop.

### Variant 7: Prestige-Comedy Version
As AI panic spreads through a fragile economy, three damaged friends convert their intelligence, instability, and mutual enabling into a boutique consultancy whose rise forces each of them to choose between loyalty, ambition, and reality.

## Usage Guidance
- Use the Primary Logline for core pitch materials and internal approvals.
- Use Variant 1 or Variant 4 for buyers, studios, and business-facing decks.
- Use Variant 2 when the leads and friendship engine need emphasis.
- Use Variant 3 or Variant 6 when pitching tone-sensitive creative partners.
- Use Variant 5 for headers, festival blurbs, and compressed metadata fields.
- Avoid versions that overstate futurism, startup glamour, or broad satire; the show's specificity is the asset.

## Do Not Say
- Do not call the show a mockumentary.
- Do not describe the trio as founders in a celebratory startup sense without also implying instability or improvisation.
- Do not pitch the AI material as speculative science fiction.
- Do not flatten the series into "friends make bad choices" without the workplace and systems angle.

## Recommended Tagline Candidates
- They made a company by mistake.
- The chat should have stayed private.
- Bad boundaries. Great deck.
- The consultancy is real. The stability is not.
- Everybody wants AI. Nobody should hire them.
